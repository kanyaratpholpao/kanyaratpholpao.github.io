Project

As part of the internship, I was assigned to create a project to develop a part disbursement system for automatic production lines or the E-Kanban system and was responsible for Concept Design, Wiring, and Program. The system is divided into 2 parts: 1. Master is in the production line, and 2. Slave is in the warehouse. The system is connected to a LAN and installed at production lines and warehouses. It can collect information on part disbursement and always updating, increasing accuracy in part disbursement, and reducing wasted resources from the production of Paper Kanban.
